VANAN KURUMBAR


Kurumbar are one of the six ancient tribal groups in Tamil Nadu. According to the Madras Census Report of 1891, the Pallavas were Kurumbas. Hunting and collecting forest produce are the two main means of living for the Kurumbar tribe.Kurumbar (also known as "Kuruba Gowda") or Gadariya, Kuruma, Kuruba, Dhangar, Pal, Baghel is a Hindu caste native to the Indian state of Karnataka,Goa, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh,Andhra Pradesh,Telangana and Tamil Nadu.
