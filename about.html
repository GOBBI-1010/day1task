Chandra clan Kshatriyas(kurumbars)


Who are we? How historical is our Kurumban community (Palangudi Makkal Kurumban) ? How special were our ancestors? Thus, without knowing the answer to many questions

we have lost the address of our society, our value, respect and honor have been hidden, and we are living without knowing ourselves. If this situation continues, the time will not be far when our posterity and our clan pride, our long historical community of Palangudi Makkal Kurumbar will be unrecognizable, ostracized and disintegrated.



1."kuru" means the guide of knowledge or light of knowledge, "pa" means the world, thus Gurupa means those who give the light of knowledge to the world, those who practice royal tricks, those who hold ministerial posts. It is not surprising that we are broken with our minds knowing that our society has been wrongly fabricated that we have only been doing the work of herding goats and cows (commodities) and are not suitable for any other profession. To realize the truth, let's look back a bit in history. Palangudi Makkal Kurumans community is an ancient, heroic, spiritual and proud race. From the time of Mahabharata till today, if we are following clan pride without any clear basis in all fields like culture, tradition, art, literature, love, family structure, we can feel that our valor and culture are mixed even in our blood.
